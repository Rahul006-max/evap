Our Story: The Evap Energies Vision

Evap Energies was founded to address a fundamental challenge in India's clean energy transition: intelligent integration. While abundant sunlight provides the foundation, true energy resilience comes from seamlessly uniting advanced solar generation with purpose-built storage and smart management into a single, optimized system.

We are not just a solar company or a battery company. We are architects of integrated clean energy ecosystems, beginning with solar-plus-storage and designed to evolve—whether scaling grid resilience, powering off-grid applications, or incorporating future vectors like green hydrogen.

Our foundation is built on the pioneering research of Dr. Altaf P, a physicist specializing in next-gen photovoltaics such as CIGS and Halide Perovskites. His work revealed that the bridge from lab discovery to commercial manufacturing was as critical as the innovation itself. This insight directly led to the creation of Evap Energies and our mission to accelerate energy technology—beginning with a proprietary halide perovskite service that slashes process optimization from months to weeks for research and pilot lines.

To engineer a complete energy system from the ground up, we integrated the groundbreaking battery research of Dr. T. Prahlada. His expertise in zinc-ion and zinc-sulfur chemistries forms the scientific core of our stationary storage solutions, which are designed for safety, sustainability, and scalability—deliberately prioritizing these essentials over the high-density but resource-constrained lithium-ion alternatives.

Guiding this scientific mission is our Chief Science Officer, Prof. R. Geetha Balakrishna, a distinguished leader in renewable energy materials whose decades of experience in research, mentorship and strategic oversight ensure that our technology roadmap is anchored in deep science and scalable innovation.

Together, we are building an integrated energy technology company. We are not just installers or importers. We are material scientists and systems engineers creating synergistic solutions—from certified perovskite precursors that accelerate industry R&D, to solar-plus-storage systems where every component is co-engineered for performance, and eventually, to our own manufactured battery packs and high-efficiency panels.

Our Three-Phase Roadmap

Phase 1: System Integration

Launch as solar-plus-storage installers for residential, commercial and industrial clients. Validate technology and build market presence.

Phase 2: Vertical Manufacturing

Begin production of proprietary zinc-based battery packs for BESS applications. Establish solar panel assembly to create fully integrated "Evap Energy Systems."

Phase 3: Product & Grid Solutions

Deploy our technology in flagship products like solar-powered EV charging stations and offer grid services using our BESS technology. Commercialize next-generation solar cells from our R&D pipeline.

Evap Energies is engineering smarter, safer, and more sustainable energy systems—where advanced solar harvesting and purpose-built storage unite to power a resilient future.
