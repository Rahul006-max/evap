Altaf Pasha, PhD

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Dr. Altaf P holds a PhD in Physics, specializing in halide perovskite photovoltaics. A US Fulbright Scholar with extensive research on CIGS PV at KIT (Germany) and Perovskite PV at Rice University (USA), he is dedicated to translating lab-scale solar innovations into commercially viable, high-efficiency products for the Indian market. His leadership embodies the synthesis of deep science and commercial execution, aiming to position EVAP as an innovation leader in India's clean energy transition.
